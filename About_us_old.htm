About

ShARE is a multicultural, non- profit organisation for students that strives to better understand the complexities of local socio-economic issues through presentations, conferences and projects. ShARE's first team was created in Shanghai in November 2002. Today, more than 500 members in Shanghai, Beijing, Hanghzhou, Seoul, New Delhi, Kolkata, Indore, Lahore, Jakarta, Lausanne, Porto, Warsaw and Paris, gather regularly to discuss economy in Asia and Europe. With the current emergence of a global knowledge and information society, ShARE long term mission is to generate, share and use knowledge to contribute to regional, human, social and economic development. ShARE IITR

The IIT Roorkee student chapter is one of the most active member of ShARE. It started in 2007. It has been recognised as one of the best teams of ShARE in the past. We being technical students, bring expertise from our own field along with socio-economic aspects.

Who we are

A ShARE member is typically an outstanding student from a top university with a strong desire to better understand the world

    University

    Prestigious university, ranking among the top 3 of the country in its field.

    Degree

    Undergraduate (at least grade 2), graduate and PhDs.

    Major

    Engineering, business, law or economics.

    Age

    19-25, 80% between 21-23.

    Nationality

    More than 15, including Chinese, Indian, Indonesian, Pakistani, French, ...

    Recruitment of a member

    Every ShARE member has been recruited by a strict selection procedure, involving a Personal Interview. The main motivation behind a student’s desire to become a ShARE member is the desire to extend and share his/her knowledge on social-economic and business issues.Our recruitment process rules out students seeking to reap benefits from our network or our contacts, or searching for a reference to add to their CVs.

Why Us
ShARE partners with World Class companies and organisations to tackle global economic, business and social issues

Present in more than 30 campuses in 15 countries, we have developed different tools and approaches to help your company strengthen its employer brand and identify talents.

STUDENT CONTEST

As competition for talents increases, classic campus promotion is not sufficient to catch students' interest. More and more MNCs are developing student contests. They are all based on the same concept, ie. confronting the students with real business situations or a technological challenge. This is an appealing concept for the students. It is a way for them to differentiate themselves from other classmates, build contact with corporate and gain experience with real-business issues.

MNCs also benefit in many ways. During the promotion of the contest, they can promote their brand. During the competition, they can build a database of talents and eventually identify and recruit talents.

ShARE has developed and launched several student contests. We worked with the office of the Nobel Prize Professor Yunus to launch a social business plan competition across 10 countries for example.

We have developed our own IT platform that can be customized and adapted. ShARE can help your company to

  • Define the concept and the strategy for the competition
  • Customize the IT solution
  • Promote the contest inside the campus
  • Manage the different phases of the contest

LECTURE/ CONFERENCE

Every year, ShARE organises a number of lectures/ conferences/ and workshops. Among other events, we have organised lectures with the World Bank Chief Economist, the CEO of EADS or the founders of the Accor Group.

Our members across the globe meet once a year during the "ShARE World Seminar" - the major event of ShARE. The location keeps changing -Shanghai, Paris, Delhi, Beijing, Bangalore but the objective remains the same - crystallising the relationships made through our network into long-lasting friendships.

Organising an event with ShARE is an efficient way for our partners to promote their brand, and also meet and discuss with talented students.

We can organise for your company

  • Large conference during our World Seminar
  • Workshop with a selection of students
  • Video conference with selected universities
  • Global chat with our members on our member access

TEAM AND NETWORK MENTORING

It is also possible for our partner to target the country, the school or the major of the students they want to reach out to, in ShARE.

Through our member access, we can connect your company to the members or the teams you want to reach. You will be able to follow their activities, receive a selection of the team outputs, promote your brand, organise workshop with the team, identify talents, offer internship or job opportunities

MANAGEMENT WORKSHOP

Every year and in every ShARE's region, we organise a meeting that gathers presidents of all our teams in the region. The presidents get trained about ShARE's latest guidelines, and together define the strategy of the region.

In parallel to this meeting, we also organise workshops between our presidents and the management team of our partners. Senior managers have few opportunities to meet and engage with students. But at the same time, they have the responsibility to grow their talent pool and identify their future managers. We offer the unique possibility to our partners to meet student leaders of major renowned universities of the region. Within just one hour meeting, senior managers can get a sense of what aspirations top talents have about their career, their ambition and about their life in general. These are critical inputs to shape the future of their organisation.

INTERVIEW OF A PARTNER
PATRICK BENAMMAR

Group Compensation, Benefits & Training Director, VALEO




What was the involvement between your company and ShARE ?

We started to be strongly connected to ShARE in 2008 at Group HR level. We have worked together on 5 projects, mainly with our Group R&D Director at the beginning and the after with our Asia Purchasing Director. In parallel, we had the chance to connect ShARE students to our China President and with our HR teams at country level in China and in India.

Why did you connect them to so high level people in your organisation? Wasn't it a waste of time for so busy people?

It is very important for Top Executives to be in contact with ShARE students as they deliver an insight of the expectations of the younger generations from the emerging world. Additionally, all students are coached to prepare the meetings and to deliver the most accurate information in an appropriate time frame.

What is so particular about ShARE compared to students association?

There is a lot to say ! The quality of the work done by the students, their engagement in the projects, the quality of coaching and methodology, their knowledge of emerging markets...strong points that are not always met by other students associations.

Has ShARE impacted your strategic decision? Can students insights be taken seriously?

ShARE projects have helped us a lot to be convinced about the necessity to implement shortly R&D activities in Asia in 2008 and 2009. The importance of the projects supported by the local governments, the quality of the research programmes shown during our exchanges, have contributed to reinforce our ambition to be a strong R&D player in China and India to support our local customers development.

Do you agree with the above positioning of ShARE? Are they so innovative and professional?

I fully agree because for a reasonable price, international students are cooperating together through ShARE projects with a high level of engagement and professionalism. On top of that, they use their creativity to help global companies to refine their strategy or to support their local operations efficiently.

What kind of topic will you work with ShARE? In which area do they have a competitive advantage?

It's very helpful for us to understand young generation's opinion and specifically in emerging markets like China or India where our business is expanding quickly. ShARE students are really connected to the world and to the decision makers and that's why their contribution is valuable for us.

From an HR point of view, why are you working with ShARE?

Working with ShARE gives us the opportunity to be directed connected to the best students from Top Universities around the world and to work directly with them on concrete projects. It's a real facilitating way for them to discover Valeo and to make our Group known from their friends and for us to identify future talent!
